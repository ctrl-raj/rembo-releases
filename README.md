# Rembo — The OS for Sports Turfs

Rembo is a unified operating system for sports turf operators. It replaces the chaos of WhatsApp-based booking management with a live operator dashboard and a player-facing mobile app.

Visit [rembo.spce](https://rembo.spcae) for more information

> "Run your turf like a serious operation."

---

## The Problem

Managing a sports turf today means bookings scattered across chats, half-remembered payments, and no-shows discovered too late. Rembo replaces that with a single dashboard and a player app that keeps everything in sync.

---

## What's in the Box

### Operator Dashboard
- Live court availability and booking management
- Real-time player verification — attendance and payment confirmation
- QR-based check-in with real-time gate updates
- 30-day rolling revenue forecasting
- Session and offer management per court
- Expiring booking alerts
- Announcements and issue tracking
- Excel / CSV data exports
- WhatsApp inquiry automation *(coming soon)*

### Player App
- Court discovery with a live availability map
- QR-scan check-in at the gate
- Booking history and management
- Subscription plan management
- Nearby court discovery
